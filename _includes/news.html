Topological data analysis preprint uploaded to bioRxiv

8 Dec 2020

Louis has uploaded the preprint “State space discovery in spatial representation circuits with persistent cohomology” to the bioRxiv. This project explores and characterizes how topological data can be applied to neuroscience data. It is a collaboration with computational topologists Dmitriy Morozov and Boyan Xu at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.
